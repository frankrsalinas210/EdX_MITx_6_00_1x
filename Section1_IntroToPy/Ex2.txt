TRUE or FALSE?

Q.1) Declarative knowledge refers to statements of fact.

A.1) True

Q.2) Imperative knowledge refers to 'how to' methods.

A.2) True

Q.3) A recipe for deducing the square root involves guessing a starting value for y. Without another recipe to be told how to pick a starting number, the computer cannot generate one on its own.

A.3) True
